Some Good Place To Go

KYND COMMUNITY ULUWATU

Jl. Labuansait No.8A, Pecatu, Kec. Kuta Sel., Kabupaten Badung, Bali

A 100% Plant-based restaurant that also supporting local farmers. The tropical and open-air design allows natural airflow while enjoying food with minim carbon footprint. Perfect destination for those looking to enjoy delicious food while living more sustainably.

HAKON ENTHIC

Setiabudi, Jakarta Selatan, Indonesia

An aesthetic matcha café combined with a mini art gallery. The semi-open structure and greenery that surround the café creating a calming, naturally ventilated atmosphere. Such an ideal spot for eco-conscious visitors who want to reduce their enviromental impact.

Taman Balai Kota Bandung

Sumur Bandung, Bandung, Indonesia

Public city park in the heart of Bandung that provides green open space for relaxation, reading, or just walking around. Spending leisure time outdoors rather than in energy-intensive environment along with the tree that help absorb CO₂.

Hutan Kota GBK

Tanah Abang, Jakarta Pusat, Indonesia

A wide urban forest park located in bustling city center. Offering shaded walking paths, picnic areas, and lush greenery, this space encourages eco-friendly habits while connecting with nature, which promote low-carbon, and sustainable lifestyle in dense urban areas.
